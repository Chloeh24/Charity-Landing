Pioneering a New Movement in Humanitarian Aid

HelpRefugees started as a hashtag in 2015. A group of friends wanted to raise funds and help the refugee crisis in Calais. Within a month we raised $50,000. Since then, we’ve supported over 80 incredible projects and helped almost 1 million people.

We believe your money should go straight to the people who need it. That’s why we make sure that 89% of your donation goes straight to the frontline.

  • – We are among the most efficient refugee charities in Europe.
  • – We have thousands of volunteers helping our cause.
  • – We’re funded almost entirely by people like you.
  • – We provide vital aid to people fleeing war, persecution and poverty.

We help where the need is greatest.

We provide emergency aid to the countries that need it the most and act where others won't. We always work with local organisations and give them what they need to help people – whether that’s funding, material aid or volunteers. We don't just provide aid, we aim to enable refugees to take control over their lives through our work.

As an independent organisation, we rely on people like you to help support our work. 89% of your donations go directly towards supporting refugees.
